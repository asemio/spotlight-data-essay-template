What is Spotlight?

Spotlight is part of Asemio’s Community Integration Platform. It delivers actionable data insights quickly by providing the tools and guidance agencies need to share data safely — without risking the privacy of the communities they serve. Foundational to the Spotlight implementation approach is the Actionable Insight Cycle, which is how we describe the path from project identification to actionable results through collaborative data sharing. The goal of this cycle is to facilitate the pursuit of knowledge through data sharing and dialogue.
